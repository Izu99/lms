## ğŸ“Š Dynamic Route Parameters

### [id] - Resource ID
Used in URLs like `/student/videos/[id]` where `[id]` is replaced with actual resource ID
Example: `/student/videos/507f1f77bcf86cd799439011`

### Common Dynamic Routes:
- Videos: `/videos/[id]`
- Papers: `/papers/[id]`
- Course Packages: `/course-packages/[id]`
- Tutes: `/tutes/[id]`

## ğŸ“ Notes

1. **Profile Page**: `/profile` is accessible by all authenticated users (students, teachers, paper managers, video managers)

2. **Dynamic Routes**: Routes with `[id]` are dynamic and require a valid MongoDB ObjectId

3. **Role-Based Access**: Each role has specific pages they can access:
   - Students: Read-only access to content
   - Teachers: Full CRUD for their content
   - Paper Managers: CRUD for papers only
   - Video Managers: CRUD for videos only

4. **Nested Routes**: Some pages have nested structures:
   - `/student/papers/[id]/results` - Paper results for specific paper
   - `/student/papers/structure-essay/[id]` - Structure/essay type papers

5. **Meeting Pages**: Both students and teachers have meeting pages for video conferencing
